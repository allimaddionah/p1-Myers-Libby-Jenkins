Exceptional Blends

Macy’s is proud to say it was one of the very first coffee roasters in the state of Arizona. We are also proud to offer only the top 5% of the worlds green coffee beans. We deal directly with the farmers and ensure that they are getting a fair price. By cutting out all the middle men, we are often able to pay better than fair trade prices. We know exactly what estates our beans come from.

We roast our beans, in house, regularly, in order to assure the freshest flavor around. We are dedicated to being the best coffee roaster in Flagstaff. Browse our online store for individual descriptions on our flavors and blends.

Holiday Selections

During the holiday season, Macy’s also offers a few other special blends such as Jamaican Blue Mountain and our Holiday blend. Due to their special nature we cannot offer them in our online store but feel free to contact us about their availability.
